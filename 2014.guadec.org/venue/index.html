Venue

GUADEC 2014 is being kindly hosted by EPITECH, a software engineering school in the heart of Strasbourg (300m away from the cathedral). It is located at 4 rue du dôme, near the Place Broglie tram station (lines B, C and F). The route from the recommended accommodation options is available is available on the accommodation page.

More about the venue

The venue building was constructed in 1791 for prince François-Charles-Joseph de Hohenlohe-Waldenburg. Former mayor of Strasbourg Étienne Livio then bought it in 1815 to live in it and the building is still known to this day as “Hôtel Livio”. It later became a brewery, then the headquarters of a bank, before it became the home of ISEG (a commerce school) in 2001, which got joined by EPITECH in 2007.
